Cribbage Assistant

This project aims to improve cribbage players' skills by providing recommendations on which cards to keep and discard to maximize scoring potential.

Developed to enhance your cribbage playing experience, this project offers a user-friendly interface where players can input their hand's six cards and receive instant recommendations.
